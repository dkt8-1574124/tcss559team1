About this project

Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) has been a popular technology and serves hundreds of customers worldwide from small enterprises to numerous large American corporations. This technology can identify significant entities such as humans, animals, or objects. RFID information brings much value to accomplish significant and complicated tasks such as event management, medical monitoring, and traffic monitoring. A significant problem in the world is the mass accumulation of garbage thrown in landfills, oceans, and natural habitats polluting the environment around us. With this project, we have created full fledged web interfaces for the waste management company view as well as an interface for the households that are clients of the company. We will talk about the objective, scope, other existing work of our RFID management system, overview of architecture, conclusions, Web API and documentation will be discussed in this report.
